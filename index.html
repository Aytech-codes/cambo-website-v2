About Combat Sambo

Combat Sambo is a dynamic martial art blending striking, throws, and submissions. Originating in 1920s Soviet Union, Combat Sambo was created to equip soldiers with a highly effective hand-to-hand fighting system. Combining Judo, Boxing, and traditional grappling techniques, it quickly became the go-to combat style for special forces and elite fighters.
